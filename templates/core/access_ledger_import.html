Access Ledger ZIP Export

Upload a .zip file exported from Access Ledger (HSoft). The ZIP should contain year folders with the entity's data files.

Expected structure:

HL_CODE.zip
├── HL_CODE_2016/
│   ├── Client.txt
│   ├── Entity.txt
│   ├── Year.txt
│   ├── Chart.txt
│   ├── Balance.txt
│   ├── AssetMaster.txt
│   └── ...
├── HL_CODE_2017/
│   └── ...
└── HL_CODE_2025/
    └── ...

What gets imported:

  • Entity details (name, ABN, TFN, type, address, directors/trustees)
  • All financial years with prior year linking for comparatives
  • Trial balance data for each year (current + prior year figures)
  • Depreciation schedules with full asset details
  • Chart of accounts with automatic account mapping
